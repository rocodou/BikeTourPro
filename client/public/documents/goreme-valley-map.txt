Göreme Valley Tour - Route Map and Details

Distance: 25 km
Duration: 3 hours
Difficulty: Easy
Starting Point: Göreme Town Center

Route Highlights:
- Göreme Open Air Museum with its ancient rock-cut churches
- Panoramic view points overlooking the fairy chimneys
- Traditional pottery workshops in Avanos
- Lunch at a local restaurant with authentic Cappadocian cuisine
- Hidden cave churches with Byzantine frescoes

This route is suitable for beginners and families, with mostly flat terrain and gentle slopes. Comfortable riding pace with frequent stops for photos and historical information.
