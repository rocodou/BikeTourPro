Red Valley Adventure - Route Map and Details

Distance: 45 km
Duration: 6 hours
Difficulty: Challenging
Starting Point: Ortahisar Village

Route Highlights:
- Red and Rose Valley's spectacular colorful rock formations
- Vineyard paths through local farms
- Ancient cave houses and churches
- Çavuşin village with its historical church
- Panoramic views from high ridges

This route is recommended for experienced riders with good fitness levels. The terrain includes steep climbs and technical descents. This adventure provides some of the most photogenic landscapes in all of Cappadocia.
